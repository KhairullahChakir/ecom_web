About ShopDemo

Learn more about our company

Our Mission

ShopDemo is a demonstration e-commerce platform built to showcase the power of real-time analytics tracking. We believe in data-driven decision making and transparent user experience optimization.

How We Track

Every page you visit, every product you view, and every action you take is being tracked by our custom-built OP-ECOM Analytics system. This data is stored in a MariaDB database and can be exported for AI/ML training.

The Technology

Our tracker uses a lightweight JavaScript client that communicates with a FastAPI backend. Session data includes page types (Administrative, Informational, ProductRelated), durations, bounce rates, and conversion events.
